About

Founder

Masoud Masoori

Masoud Masoori is an AI/ML engineer and builder with a focus on hands-on experimentation and efficiency-first approaches to machine learning.

With a background in developing production AI systems, Masoud is passionate about reducing the computational and energy costs of NLP models while maintaining performance. NatureNLP represents a research prototype exploring nature-inspired computation principles for sustainable AI.

The project emphasizes architectural innovations that can be adopted by larger models, focusing on training-time and inference-time efficiency through oscillatory mechanisms, sparsity, and regenerative learning patterns.
